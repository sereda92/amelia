How to order?

Step 1

YOU CHOOSE A PAINTING THAT YOU LIKE

It is important to carefully select a painting. Understand that a painting is a purchase that will always be with you. Therefore, it is crucial that it appeals to all members of your family.

Step 2

I VERIFY IF THE PAINTING IS AVAILABLE

Checking the availability or reservation of the painting is important to ensure that it is for sale and that you will be able to purchase it.

Step 3

YYOU MAKE A DEPOSIT FOR THE PAINTING

A deposit of 50% of the painting's price reserves it for you and safeguards us against potential risks of losing the painting and money.

Step 4

I SEND YOU THE PACKAGED PAINTING

Before shipping, the painting must be carefully packaged in three layers of different materials to preserve its quality and protect it from possible damage.

Step 5

YOU RECEIVE THE PAINTING

On average, the painting is delivered within 10-14 days. Upon receiving the painting, you make the second payment of 50%.

Step 6

WE DELIGHT IN THE RESULT

Now it's time to enjoy the result. Appreciate the painting and let it bring you joy and satisfaction. Please note that delivery times may vary.
